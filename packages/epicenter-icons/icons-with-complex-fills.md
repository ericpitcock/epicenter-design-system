## Key Insights

1. **Mixed-Mode Icons**: About 3.8% (172/4564) of Hugeicons use complex filled paths alongside stroke elements
2. **Common Patterns**: These are typically icons representing:
   - Complex shapes that need solid fills (arrows, UI elements)
   - Objects with internal details (technology devices, clothing)
   - Icons requiring visual emphasis (validation, status indicators)
3. **Design Philosophy**: Hugeicons uses intelligent mixed rendering where:
   - Simple lines and borders remain stroked for clarity
   - Complex internal shapes are pre-outlined and filled for visual impact
4. **Categories Most Affected**: AI/Technology, Arrows, Security, and Business icons frequently use this mixed approach

This list demonstrates why our intelligent CSS system (respecting `fill="currentColor"` vs stroke) is crucial for proper icon display.
